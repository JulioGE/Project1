< About Us "Heighten" was created by four students(Jen, John, Julio, Sha) while attending Rutgers Coding Bootcamp in Somerset for their 1st project. We wanted to create a website where an individual can "Heighten" their mood with music(Spotify API), directions to nearest marijuana dispensary(Odreeba API), and provide recommendations on new places to grab some grub on the route(Food API). We wanted to provide a service where you have options all in one place. Feel free to enjoy the website. Please be safe and enjoy.
